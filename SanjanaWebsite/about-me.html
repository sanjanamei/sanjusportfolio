About me

I’m a student of creative digital media, and I plan to pursue a career in UI/UX design. I love exploring different types of media related to art & creativity. I also love using pastel colours in my work, as they add a more calming effect to the art. Ever since I was a young girl, I had a passion for creativity and art, whether it be through music, clothes, graphic design or code.
